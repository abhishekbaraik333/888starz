Agent Requirements

The basic rules that must be agreed with the agent upon registration in order to avoid misunderstanding and reduce fraud risks:

The agent must be at least 18 years old

The agent must provide the company with a photo of his ID, passport or driver's license in order to verify his identity

The main task of the agent is to faithfully replenish players' accounts and withdraw players' funds, which allows the agent to earn commissions from replenishment and withdrawal

The agent must have a team to ensure transactions are processed 24/7

The agent must have the necessary equipment to process transactions conveniently and quickly

The agent undertakes not to engage in activities that are contrary to the company's rules. If the agent becomes aware that the player is intentionally violating the company rules, the agent undertakes to report the violations to the company managers
